How to use
Five steps to a stronger interview
01

Open the dashboard

Click "Open Dashboard". Allow camera and microphone when the browser asks — both are needed for recording and analysis.

02

Pick a question

Go to the Question Bank tab. Browse 20+ real interview questions with answer scripts. Click "Use this question" to load it into your session.

03

Record your answer

Press Record and answer out loud. The app analyses your face expressions, voice fluency, and transcribes every word in real time.

04

Review and paraphrase

Go to Recordings → Review. See your full breakdown, watch the video back, and get an AI-paraphrased version of your answer showing how to say it better.

05

Improve and repeat

The timeline shows which parts of your answer need work. Practise again, delete old recordings, and track your score improving over time.
