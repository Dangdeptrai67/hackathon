Before you record — read this
◈ Quiet room required. Background noise gets picked up as speech. A loud room will ruin your transcript and give you a 0% fluency score.
◈ Camera must be allowed. Click "Allow" when your browser asks for camera and microphone access. Without it, nothing works.
◈ Use Chrome browser. Speech recognition and face detection only work fully in Chrome. Firefox and Safari will miss features.
◈ Speak directly at the mic. Sit 30–60cm from your device. Speak clearly and at a normal volume — not too fast, not too quiet.
◈ Face the camera squarely. Face AI needs your face centred and well-lit. Avoid sitting with a bright window behind you.
◈ Answer the question shown. The app checks if your answer is relevant. Random words or background noise will be flagged as off-topic.
